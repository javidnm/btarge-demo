BTAFLOW Business Process Management enables easy transition to electronic workflow, efficiency to be increased in a short time and process costs to be reduced.
BTAFLOW provides automated design and automation of business processes without coding through graphical tools, and flexible workflow automation solutions that institutions need with many features it offers.

Features of the Application

Automatic reminder, Warning ve Escalation Management

  • Time definitions by job
  • Online warning and escalation in delays (Online email and sms)
  • Integration with Outlook

Access to all data from one screen

  • Access to all the information required for the completion of that job through the form in which the job was performed
  • Keeping track of jobs on mobile compatible screens due to being a responsive application

Automation of Workflow

  • All processes are interconnected; every job automatically triggers the next
  • No more searching through the menus
  • List of jobs that everyone will follow on their own screen

Online Tracking of Business Processes

  • Tracking the waiting times of the works in steps
  • Detection of process congestion points and possibility of immediate intervention
  • Opportunity to provide instant and accurate information in customer service

BTACMS

- content management system which is easy to use and able to manage all environments with one control center. Through the BTACMS Content Management System, which operates independently from the platform, the modules are managed and used simultaneously from all devices (computer, phone, tablet) with a single control center.
BTACMS content management system allows to create, edit and publish text, embedded audio and video files and digital content such as interactive graphics for users' websites, mobile and portal applications. This system simplifies loading and writing content for users who do not have coding skills.

Benefits of BTACMS Content Management System to institutions

Saving on time

  • BTACMS has time-saving features for users. It's easy to observe what it can do, from built-in page templates to localizing content, from page analysis to contextual editing.

Income Growth

  • Marketers can use a CMS to generate revenue from campaigns they create by continuing to generate demand through content. With BTACMS Content Management system , it is easy to integrate to a Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system.

More Effective Content

  • A CMS provides detailed metrics for each page as well as site-wide analysis. In addition to metrics such as page views, page view time, and bounce rate, it provides meaningful information about your site, such as top performance pages, trends, visitor flows, and conversion paths.

High Efficiency

  • When marketers manage content without help from IT, it makes them more productive. They will keep their campaigns under full control from content to timing and results.

Power BI

Data Integration with Power BI
